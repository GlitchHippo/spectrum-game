How to Play
1 of 3
Find the 5 that belong.
You're given 7 items. Five share a hidden theme — two are decoys. Select exactly 5 and submit. A wrong pick costs a life. You have 4 lives total.
Example — theme: "Things with holes"
Donut
Sponge
Cheese
Apple ✗
Macaroni
Bowling Ball
Baseball ✗
2 of 3
Now put them in order.
The 5 items share a hidden ranking — your job is to figure out what it is and sort them lowest to highest. Tap any two items to swap, then submit.
Example — the hidden scale was: size
Macaroni✓ correct
Donut✓ correct

Reading the feedback.
After each Phase 2 guess, you get one colored tile per item showing how close that position was.
✓
Correct — exactly the right spot
~
Close — off by exactly one position
✗
Wrong — more than one spot off
