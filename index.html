How to Play
1 of 3
Find the 5 that belong.
You're given 7 items. Five share a hidden theme — two are decoys. Select exactly 5 and submit. A wrong pick costs a life.
Example — theme: "Things with holes"
Donut
Sponge
Cheese
Apple ✗
Macaroni
Bowling Ball
Baseball ✗
2 of 3
Now put them in order.
The 5 items can be ranked on a hidden scale — lowest to highest. You have to figure out what that scale is. Tap any two items to swap their positions, then submit.
Example — you figured out: size
Macaroni smallest
Donut
Sponge off by 1
Cheese off by 1
Bowling Ball largest
3 of 3
Reading the feedback.
After each Phase 2 guess you'll see colored tiles — one per item — showing how close each position was.
✓
Correct — this item is in exactly the right spot
~
Close — off by exactly one position
✗
Wrong — more than one spot off
A new puzzle drops every day. Your progress and stats are saved automatically.
